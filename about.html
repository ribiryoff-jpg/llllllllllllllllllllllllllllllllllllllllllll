Get Started

We're building the future of web creation.

Zexet was born from a simple idea: what if creating for the web was as easy as having a conversation? We're a team of developers and designers passionate about making web development accessible to everyone, from solo creators to enterprise teams.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate the friction between idea and execution. We believe that creativity shouldn't be limited by technical skill. Zexet empowers you to bring your vision to life by simply describing it, allowing our AI agent to handle the complexities of code. We're not just building a tool; we're creating a partner for your creative process.

How It Works

Zexet leverages state-of-the-art Generative AI models to understand your requests. Here’s the magic behind it:

  • Contextual Understanding

    The AI doesn't just see your prompt; it sees your entire project. It understands the existing code, file structure, and past changes to make intelligent, context-aware modifications.

  • Clean, Semantic Code

    We've trained our agent to prioritize quality. It generates production-ready HTML and Tailwind CSS that is responsive, accessible, and easy to maintain. No messy, unreadable code.

  • Iterative Refinement

    Zexet excels at iteration. Start with a broad idea, then refine every detail through conversation. From changing a button color to restructuring an entire section, the process is fluid and intuitive.
